Clan Protocols & Rules

To maintain a healthy, competitive, and active clan environment, every member must follow these rules. Continuous violations may lead to **warnings**, **demotions**, or **removal**.

  • 1. Weekly Donations Required

    All members must contribute regularly. Zero donations for the week results in a warning.

  • 2. Maintain Average Trophy Standards

    Members must maintain trophies close to the clan average. Very low trophies affect activity score.

  • 3. Be Active & Participate

    Engage in clan activities, wars, and capital raids. Inactive players fall into nomination list.

  • 4. Respect the Community

    Toxic behavior, fighting, or disrespect towards clan members will not be tolerated.

  • 5. Elders Must Contribute

    Elders who fail to donate or contribute regularly will be **demoted automatically**.

  • 6. Removal Based on Score

    Members with continuous low contributions enter Week 1 nomination list; Week 2 they are removed.
